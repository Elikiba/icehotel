ABOUT MARY

Adeola Bamidele, born in 2003 in Ibadan, Nigeria, is an ambitious Theatre Arts student at the University of Ibadan, stepping into the spotlight as a contestant on The Next Movie Star Show. Hailing from Oyo State, Adeola's passion for acting ignited through school plays and local drama clubs, where their commanding stage presence and knack for emotional depth quickly stood out. Now in their third year of study, they've honed their craft in university productions, mastering everything from intense monologues to lively dance routines infused with Yoruba flair. Adeola's drive to join Nollywood stems from a love for storytelling and a dream to inspire others, fueled by influences like Funke Akindele and Tunde Kelani. With a bold yet grounded approach, they aim to impress the judges with originality and a fierce work ethic, seeing the show as a launchpad to transform their student-stage talent into a cinematic legacy.
